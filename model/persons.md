## Persons

A Person changes her email. A Person makes a **Change Email Request**, receives a
validation message to new email address, then confirms new address. If new address is confirmed
successfully, email of the Person changes. Change Email Request expires if it is not confirmed
within configured timespan.

A Person changes her password. The Person enters old password, double new password, after submission,
password is changed if provided passwords match.

A Person resets her password. The Person specifies registered email and requests *password change*.
A password change link is sent to specified email. The Person follows the link, enters double
new password, after submission, new password is set for the Person.
